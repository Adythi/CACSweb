About Us

The Best IT Solution With Vast Experience

At Command and Control Solutions Corp., based in New York's Tech Valley, we're pioneers in delivering innovative and strategic technology services and solutions. Focusing on reliability, security, and cost-effectiveness, and with a diverse client base spanning small businesses to federal enterprises, our broad spectrum of services, from executive consulting and IT strategy alignment to advanced cybersecurity and infrastructure management, have provided outstanding results for over thirteen years. Underpinned by a team of highly-skilled professionals and led by our CEO and COO executive leaders, both US Military Academy West Point graduates, we're committed to transparent, flawless excellence in project execution and outstanding customer satisfaction. Entrust us to be your partner in command of your technology solution and control of your IT infrastructure.
